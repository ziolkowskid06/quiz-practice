      accept: ["Responsible Accountable Consult Inform", "raci"]

      question: "WBS"
      answer: "Work Breakdown Structure"
      accept: ["Work Breakdown Structure", "work breakdown structure"]
      explanation: "A hierarchical decomposition of the total scope of work to be carried out by the project team to accomplish the project objectives and create the required deliverables."

    - id: 4
      type: fill-blank
      question: "CBS"
      answer: "Cost Breakdown Structure"
      accept: ["Cost Breakdown Structure"]
      explanation: "A hierarchical breakdown of project costs by category (e.g., labor, materials, equipment). It allows for detailed financial tracking and reporting."

    - id: 5
      type: fill-blank
      question: "CWBS"
      answer: "Contract Work Breakdown Structure"
      accept: ["Contract Work Breakdown Structure"]
      explanation: "The specific portion of the WBS that is defined and negotiated within a legal contract between a buyer and a seller."

    - id: 6
      type: fill-blank
      question: "OBS"
      answer: "Organizational Breakdown Structure"
      accept: ["Organizational Breakdown Structure"]
      explanation: "A hierarchical representation of the project organization, used to assign functional responsibility for specific work packages to specific departments or teams."

    - id: 7
      type: fill-blank
      question: "PBS"
      answer: "Product Breakdown Structure"
      accept: ["Product Breakdown Structure"]
      explanation: "Similar to a WBS, but focused entirely on the physical components of the product rather than the work required to create them. Commonly used in PRINCE2 environments."

    - id: 8
      type: fill-blank
      question: "RAM"
      answer: "Responsibility Assignment Matrix"
      accept: ["Responsibility Assignment Matrix"]
      explanation: "A grid that shows project resources assigned to each work package. It clarifies who is working on what, ensuring no overlap or missing assignments."

    - id: 9
      type: fill-blank
      question: "IPT"
      answer: "Integrated Project Team"
      accept: ["Integrated Project Team"]
      explanation: "A multidisciplinary group of people who are collectively responsible for delivering a product. This is a precursor to the modern Agile 'Cross-functional' team."

    - id: 10

      question: "LRC"
      answer: "Linear Responsibility Chart"
      accept: ["Linear Responsibility Chart"]
      explanation: "Another name for a Responsibility Assignment Matrix (RAM). It clearly maps out who is responsible for which activities, ensuring accountability across the team."

    - id: 11
      type: fill-blank
      question: "RBS"
      answer: "Resource Breakdown Structure / Risk Breakdown Structure"
      accept: ["Resource Breakdown Structure", "Risk Breakdown Structure", "resource breakdown structure", "risk breakdown structure"]
      explanation: "A dual-use acronym. As a Resource Breakdown Structure: a hierarchical list of resources by category and type. As a Risk Breakdown Structure: a hierarchical decomposition of risk categories that helps the team systematically identify and organize project risks."
